## What is Capx?

Capx (capx.ai) is infrastructure for agent-run companies — deploy autonomous businesses via typed playbooks, multi-agent orchestration, governance, and on-chain settlement. It's a developer/founder-facing product living in the crypto + AI-agent space.

---

## Core Product Features

1. **Typed playbooks** — structured, machine-readable definitions of how an autonomous business operates, so agents execute against a spec instead of freeform prompts.
2. **Multi-agent orchestration** — coordination layer for multiple agents working together on complex workflows.
3. **Governance** — controls and oversight for how agent-run companies make and execute decisions.
4. **On-chain settlement** — value transfer and settlement for agent-run businesses happens on-chain.

## Key Messaging / Narrative Pillars

> These are the angles personas can take when talking about Capx. Each persona should lean into 1-2 of these.

1. **Agent-Run Companies:** The shift from software-as-a-tool to businesses that run themselves — agents that operate, transact, and grow a company with humans setting direction.
2. **Typed Playbooks:** Reliability angle — autonomous businesses need specs, not vibes. Typed playbooks make agent behavior predictable and auditable.
3. **Multi-Agent Orchestration:** One agent is a demo; a coordinated team of agents is a company. The interesting engineering is in the orchestration.
4. **On-Chain Settlement:** Agents need a native way to hold and move value; on-chain rails give agent businesses programmable, transparent settlement.
5. **Developer/Founder Infrastructure:** Capx is picks-and-shovels for people building agent businesses, not a consumer app.
